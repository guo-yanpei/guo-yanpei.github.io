About Me

Hi! I am a PhD student in Computer Science at National University of Singapore starting in Jan. 2025, fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jiaheng Zhang.

My current research interests lie in applied cryptography, with a particular focus on zero-knowledge proofs and succinct non-interactive arguments of knowledge (SNARKs).

I am always open to collaborations. If you share similar interests, feel free to email me.
